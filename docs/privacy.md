## The Short Version

KeySmith does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your data. Ever.

## Data Collection

KeySmith collects **zero** data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected
- **No usage analytics** or telemetry
- **No crash reporting** to external services
- **No advertising identifiers**
- **No network requests** of any kind
- **No cookies or tracking pixels**

## Password Storage

Passwords you save to the vault are stored locally on your device using the iOS Keychain, which is encrypted by the Secure Enclave hardware. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is protected by your device passcode and/or biometrics (Face ID / Touch ID)
- Is not included in unencrypted backups
- Cannot be accessed by other apps

## Keyboard Extension

The KeySmith keyboard extension requests "Full Access" solely to enable clipboard functionality (copying generated passwords). Even with Full Access enabled:

- No keystrokes are recorded or transmitted
- No text input data is collected
- No network connections are made
- The keyboard operates entirely offline

## Third-Party Services

KeySmith uses **no** third-party services, SDKs, frameworks, or analytics tools.

## Children's Privacy

KeySmith does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
